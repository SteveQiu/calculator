## Technical Decisions

- `SharedFlow<Result>` for cross-layer event delivery (no callback leaks)
- `pendingUnlockTheme` on ViewModel to bridge AdRepository's generic reward callback
- `backgroundTintList` for programmatic MaterialButton coloring (preserves shape)
- `stringSetPreferencesKey` for unlocked themes (future-proof, no per-theme migration)
- `ThemeId.toColors(context)` reads from R.color.* (single source of truth in colors.xml)
